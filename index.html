AresIS

The galaxy's first Areographical Information System for Martian cartography.


Features

  • Consistent cartographic representation and symbology. No Martian topology will ever get in the way of your data's integrity!
  • Persistent time-logging on all incoming data and changes. Don't let relativity get in the way of your decision making!
  • Areoanalytical tools and algorithms independent from Terrestrially-trained processes. Let your Martian settlement be a first for humanity!

Pricing

Rover

  • Connect our software to your rover and knit images together.
  • Images are automatically areoreferenced.
  • Time stamps keep your images as valid data.

Explorer

  • Develop coordinate systems for your Martian imagery
  • Get data layers delivered to your Terrestrial computers. Learn about Martian dust in real time!
  • Map the surface of your quadrant before a human even sets foot on the Red Planet.

Settler

  • Find optimal locations for habitat building.
  • Map space routes and orbital patterns to touch down your space shuttle.
  • Connect to orbital satellites to monitor for dust storms and protect your personnel.
